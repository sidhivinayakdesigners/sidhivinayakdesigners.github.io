Luxury Apartment Interior Ideas for Premium Living

Home / Blog / Article
2026-05-28 5 Min Read

Creating a luxury interior is about selecting statement materials and maintaining a cohesive design language. Here is how to achieve a ?50 lakh premium aesthetic in a modern apartment.

1. Premium Stone and Translucent Marble

A great way to create a premium focal point is using a backlit Onyx marble slab or a large-format grey sintered stone tile behind your TV console. This eliminates the need for busy display cases, making the wall look clean and grand.

2. Tinted Glass & Gold Profile Wardrobes

Replace solid wardrobe doors with bronze-tinted glass panel shutters fitted inside gold aluminum profiles. Adding vertical LED warm strip bars inside the wardrobes makes the closet look like a luxury boutique display.

3. Integrated Home Automation

Incorporate smart switches to control lights, curtains, and motorized cabinets via voice commands or mobile apps. Automated drawers that slide open with a simple tap on the modular kitchen facade add immense convenience.
