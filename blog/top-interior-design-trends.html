Top Interior Design Trends Shaping Modern Homes

Home / Blog / Article
2026-06-18 4 Min Read

Interior trends are shifting away from stark cold minimalism toward warm, cozy luxury. Homeowners in Navi Mumbai are looking for spaces that are functional, elegant, and visually calm.

1. Warm Metallics and Gold Accents

Brushed gold, brass, and bronze profile inlays are replacing traditional silver steel. Integrating gold metallic borders inside veneer wall panels or TV cabinets instantly elevates the room's luxurious look without cluttering the floor space.

2. Linear Wall Panelings & Louvers

Fluted wall panels and wooden louvers are being widely used to hide structural columns or create statement partition dividers between living and dining halls. They add a tactile, textured quality that breaks up flat drywall fields.

3. Magnetic Track Profiles

Standard bulb holders are out. Modern homes use recessed magnetic tracks that allow homeowners to clip on, slide, and rotate different lighting modules (spotlights, linear diffuse bars, pendant accents) dynamically.
