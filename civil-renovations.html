Structural Modifications & Waterproofing

We deliver heavy-duty civil masonry, structural alterations, premium tiling, and specialized bathroom waterproofing. Our contractors have over 25 years of experience in structural modifications, ensuring your project is safe, clean, and compliant.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Wall Alterations: Precision wall demolitions, partition brickwork masonry, and structural column column wraps.
  • Tile & Marble Laying: Ceramic, vitrified, or large-format Italian marble slab flooring and wall cladding installations.
  • Kitchen Platform Work: Granite shifting, L-shaped platform re-alignment, quartz molding, and sink drainage plumbing adjustments.
  • Bathroom Waterproofing: Comprehensive hacking, double-coat liquid waterproofing membrane application, leakage tests, and re-tiling.
  • Plastering & Levelling: High-density river sand plastering and self-levelling concrete layouts for flooring bases.
