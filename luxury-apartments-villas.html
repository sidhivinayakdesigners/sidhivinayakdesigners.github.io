Luxury Bedrooms & Wardrobes

Home / Services / Luxury Bedrooms

Premium Master Suites & Custom Headboards

We deliver high-end master bedroom designs focusing on bespoke luxury, custom texturing, and top-tier finishes. Each room configuration is designed using imported materials, natural wood veneers, and hydraulic storage bed assemblies.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Floor-to-Ceiling Wardrobes: Extra height openable or sliding wardrobe units with PVD gold metal profiles and soft-close hardware.
  • Bespoke Headboards: Wall-sized leatherette or velvet cushioned headboards with warm rear LED strip channels.
  • Vanity Integrations: Custom floating dressing tables, sensor-activated mirrors, and jewelry cabinets.
  • Lighting Schemes: Concealed cove LED lighting, crystal drop pendants, and side table reading lights.
  • Wall Textures: Imported wall finishes, metallic stucco finishes, premium wood panels, and custom wallpaper detailing.

Material Specifications

Component Luxury Specific Materials
Base Material 18mm BWP Plywood (Boiling Water Proof)
External Finish Natural veneers finished with high-end PU polish coatings or premium acrylic sheets
Drawer Guides Under-mount soft-close slides with motion damping (Hettich / Blum)
Bed Mechanism Hydraulic lift pumps supporting up to 150kg load (Ebco)
Metal Profiling PVD coated titanium gold or rose gold profile inserts
