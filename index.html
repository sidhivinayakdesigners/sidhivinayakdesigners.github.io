Navi Mumbai Interior Design Guide & FAQ

At Siddhivinayak Designers, we believe in complete transparency. Here is our comprehensive guide to residential design, modular layouts, false ceilings, and interior contracting services in Navi Mumbai.

The execution cost varies based on materials and layout scope. A standard 1 BHK apartment turnkey package (including basic modular kitchen, false ceilings, laminate wardrobes, and paint) starts from ₹3.5 Lakhs. Luxury 2 BHK and 3 BHK duplexes range between ₹5.5 Lakhs to ₹12 Lakhs depending on premium finishes like acrylic shutters, quartz countertops, or custom veneer paneling.

BWP (Boiling Water Proof) Marine Grade Plywood (conforming to IS:710 standards) is highly moisture-resistant and resists decay in wet areas. Unlike standard commercial MR-grade plywood or MDF, BWP plywood does not swell, warp, or attract termites under sink units or damp pantry cabinets, ensuring your modular kitchen lasts for decades.

A standalone interior designer only provides 2D/3D layouts and material advice, leaving you to manage separate painters, carpenters, and false ceiling mason teams. Siddhivinayak Designers operates on a turnkey interior contracting model, meaning we take absolute responsibility for both design and complete execution. Our master contractors supervise every detail, from raw brickwork modifications to final soft-close hardware installations, saving you time and preventing costly mistakes.

Modular kitchens and bedroom wardrobe packages are typically delivered and installed within 30 to 35 days from layout sign-off, as components are manufactured off-site. A complete turnkey home renovation (including false ceilings, electrical rewiring, masonry, tiling, and modular fabrication) takes between 45 to 60 days depending on the structural scale.
