Turnkey Contracting Solutions

Home / Services / Turnkey Contracting

Single-Window Execution Model

We act as your master contractors, handling all aspects of civil construction, masonry, structural alterations, electrical loads, and plumbing routing. By managing all specialized agencies in-house, we guarantee quality and clean deadlines.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Zoning & Layouts: Comprehensive architectural mapping, detailed wall layouts, and electrical/plumbing routing schemes.
  • Civil & Masonry: Wall relocations, kitchen counter reshaping, structural plastering, waterproofing, and tile laying.
  • Power Load Balancing: Fire-resistant wire routing, main DB board installations, power load calculations, and switchgear allocations.
  • Plumbing Routing: High-pressure water lines, concealed flush tanks mounting, and drainage plumbing layouts.
  • Supervision & Approvals: Technical coordinator on-site, society approvals assistance, and work safety certification.
