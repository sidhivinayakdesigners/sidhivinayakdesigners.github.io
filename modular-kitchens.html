Ergonomic Modular Kitchens

We manufacture and execute premium modular kitchens tailored for maximum efficiency and style. Each kitchen is manufactured using boiling-waterproof marine plywood and installed with branded hardware configurations to survive high humidity and continuous daily usage.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Flexible Layouts: L-Shaped, U-Shaped, Parallel, and customized Island layout design and fitting.
  • Storage Accessories: Smart tandem boxes, pull-out spice racks, corner carousel baskets, and cutlery organizers.
  • Finishes & Shutters: Premium scratch-resistant acrylic, textured laminates, or anti-fingerprint PU options.
  • Countertops: High-density granite or stain-resistant quartz platform custom moldings.
  • Appliance Fitting: Chimney and hob cutouts, built-in microwave housings, and dedicated refrigerator slots.

Material Specifications

Component Kitchen Specific Materials
Cabinet Core 18mm BWP IS:710 Marine Grade Plywood (Boiling Water Proof)
Hardware Soft-close drawers, hinges, and lift-up systems (Blum / Hafele)
Cabinet Liner 0.8mm Inner PVC matching white/gray laminates for easy cleaning
Shutter Finish 1.5mm high-gloss anti-scratch Acrylic or customized PU finish
Hinges & Tracks Stainless Steel SS 304 soft-close hinges to resist rust
