High-Impact Retail Outlets & Storefronts

We design high-traffic retail spaces designed to maximize product exposure and customer circulation. From luxury display counters to structural glass facades, we manage the entire project to ensure your brand stands out.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Display Counters: Glass-topped wooden display cases, integrated warm lighting strips, drawer locks, and custom storage bases.
  • Product Racks: Heavy-duty shelving, customized wall paneling, acrylic/metal brackets, and branding banners integration.
  • Storefront Facades: Toughened frameless glass panels, large steel hinges, and custom entrance handles.
  • Showroom Lighting: Adjustable track spotlight rails, directed LED downlights, and warm focal lights to emphasize products.
  • Billing & Cash Desks: Compact billing tables, POS cabinet wire layouts, security locks, and logo acrylic panels.

Material Specifications

Component Retail Specific Materials
Wood Substrate 18mm BWR (Boiling Water Resistant) Plywood (CenturyPly / Greenply)
Counter Tops 1.2mm high-gloss wear-resistant laminates or solid acrylic surfaces (Corian)
Glass Panels 10mm or 12mm clear toughened glass with polished bevel edges
Locks & Tracks High-security central lock systems and heavy telescopic channels (Ebco / Godrej)
Spotlighting Warm-white track LED spots (15W/20W) with flexible directional pivot bases (Syska)
