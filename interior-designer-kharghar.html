Civil Work, Structural Demolition & CIDCO Safety Clearances

Navi Mumbai structural guidelines require strict safety checks. If you are planning load redistribution or removing brick partition walls in your Kharghar flat, it is critical to obtain CIDCO (City and Industrial Development Corporation) structural safety clearances. Siddhivinayak Designers handles this paperwork for you. We provide certified structural stability designs and coordinate with licensed engineers to verify structural viability before any sledgehammer hits the wall.

Our civil execution services include advanced waterproofing (crucial for flats facing Navi Mumbai's heavy monsoon winds and humidity). We utilize a multi-layered waterproofing system using Dr. Fixit chemical solutions for kitchen zones, bathroom floors, and balconies. We replace outdated pipes with high-durability CPVC lines to eliminate leakage risks permanently.

Material Selection & Brand Spec Sheets

We believe in complete transparency. Our detailed Bill of Quantities (BOQ) specifies the exact brands and materials that will go into your home. Here is our default brand specification list for premium projects:

  • Structural Plywood: CenturyPly (Sainik / Club Prime) or Greenply IS:710 certified BWP Marine Plywood. We never use MDF or particle board for structural framing.
  • Modular Shutters: Premium 1.2mm anti-scratch acrylic finishes or Royale Touche natural wood veneers.
  • Hardware & Channels: Hettich (Sensys soft-close hinges) or Blum (motorized drawer runner slides) carrying lifetime warranties.
  • Waterproofing Coatings: Dr. Fixit 301 LW+ structural admixture and Super Latex waterproofing seals.
  • Wall Finishing System: Asian Paints Royale Luxury Emulsion or Dulux Velvet Touch paints over double-coat acrylic wall putty.

False Ceiling Designs & Lighting Layouts

A false ceiling does more than just conceal electric conduits; it acts as a primary design layer that controls the visual height and atmosphere of a room. For our projects in Kharghar, we install Gyproc gypsum plasterboards to prevent ceiling cracking. Our electrical engineers plan lighting distributions utilizing indirect LED strip panels (cove lighting) and recessed black magnetic tracks.

These track lighting systems allow you to slide, rotate, or exchange spotlight panels according to your furniture layout shifts, giving you total flexibility. We integrate automated dimming controllers to let you switch between bright functional light and cozy warm ambient settings with a click.
