Custom Wardrobes & Upholstered Bedroom Details

We craft bespoke bedroom interiors focusing on spatial logic, generous storage, and luxurious comfort. From slide-to-open heavy wardrobes to custom headboards, each piece is fabricated using durable MR/BWP grade plywood to ensure longevity and robust utility.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Custom Wardrobes: Sliding door mechanisms, traditional hinges, floor-to-ceiling heights, and integrated loft modules.
  • Bespoke Bed Frames: Hydraulic storage support, customized headboards (cushioned/paneling), and side tables.
  • Integrated Vanity: Dressing table with LED border vanity mirrors, jewelry organizer drawers, and accent seating.
  • Ambient Lighting: Under-bed warm neon strips, internal sensor wardrobe LEDs, and study desk lighting loops.
  • Paneling Details: Charcoal louvers, veneered backdrops, fabric padding, and accent molding frames.

Material Specifications

Component Bedroom Specific Materials
Base Material 18mm MR (Moisture Resistant) Grade Commercial Plywood
External Finish 1mm Matte/Glossy Laminates (Century / Merino) or Natural Veneers
Internal Liner 0.8mm White or Textured Fabric-feel Laminates
Sliding System Heavy-duty top-hanging sliding door track channels (Ebco / Hettich)
Sensor Lighting Automatic motion-sensor LED profile lights inside wardrobe drawers
