Bespoke Turnkey Interior Contracting

We handle every phase of your project. Our in-house contracting team manages structural alterations, tiling, plumbing updates, gypsum false ceilings, electrical work, and modular cabinet installation. A single point of contact coordinates all teams and keeps you updated weekly.

Civil Work & CIDCO Clearances

We design according to Navi Mumbai building guidelines. For structural revisions or brick wall demolition, we coordinate with licensed engineers to verify safety and stability. Our contracting service also includes multi-layer waterproofing for kitchens, bathrooms, and balconies using Dr. Fixit admixtures.

Custom Modular Kitchens & Wardrobes

We customize modern layouts using BWR plywood, acrylic finishes, and soft-close German hardware (Hettich/Hafele). Custom manufacturing takes place in our automated workshop, guaranteeing a precise fit and an on-time 45-day site delivery timeline.

Material Selection & Brand Spec Sheets

Every quotation features a transparent Bill of Quantities (BOQ) detailing raw material brand specs:

  • Plywood: CenturyPly or Greenply IS:710 certified BWP Marine Plywood.
  • Fittings: Hettich or Blum soft-close runner slides and hinges.
  • Waterproofing: Dr. Fixit chemical structural coatings.
  • Paint: Asian Paints Royale or Dulux Velvet Touch over double-coat putty.

False Ceiling & Lighting Layouts

We install crack-resistant Gyproc false ceilings integrated with cove lighting and adjustable magnetic track lights, providing beautiful, flexible layouts that control the atmosphere of your room.
