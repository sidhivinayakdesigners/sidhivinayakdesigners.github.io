Complete Home Interior

Home / Services / Complete Home Interior

Turnkey 1BHK, 2BHK & 3BHK Home Solutions

We deliver complete, hassle-free turnkey home interior design and execution solutions in Koparkhairne and Navi Mumbai, transforming your empty shell into a premium, luxury living space fully managed by our in-house contracting team.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Living Room Design: Customized large format TV units, wall paneling, partitions, and modular glass displays.
  • Modular Kitchen: Highly functional acrylic/laminate modular setups with high-quality soft-close hinges.
  • Bedrooms & Wardrobes: Custom floor-to-ceiling wardrobes (sliding or openable), bespoke beds, and headboards.
  • Ceilings & Lighting: Gypsum board false ceilings with warm cove LED lighting, track lights, and chandeliers.
  • Electrical & Civil Works: Power outlets shifting, plumbing modifications, bathroom renovations, tiling, and painting.
