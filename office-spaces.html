Corporate & Commercial Workspaces

We design and execute modern, highly functional corporate offices focusing on ergonomics, durable materials, and professional branding integrations. From executive cabins to collaborative desks, we manage the entire turnkey setup including network cabling and electrical layouts.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Executive Cabins: Large executive tables, credenzas, storage racks, glass partitions, and visitor seating setups.
  • Staff Workstations: Linear/Cluster desks, integrated cable management, acoustic divider screens, and ergonomic mesh chairs.
  • Conference Rooms: Audio-visual projection walls, large modular conference tables, soundproofing partitions, and presentation boards.
  • Reception & Lobbies: Custom reception counters, acrylic brand logo backings, waiting sofas, and accent ceiling details.
  • Networking & Power: Server rack positioning, CAT6 data cabling, electrical load distribution, and UPS power backups routing.

Material Specifications

Component Office Specific Materials
Wood Substrate 18mm Commercial Grade MR Plywood or Fire-Retardant MDF (Century / Greenpanel)
Laminate Layer 1.2mm industrial high-wear laminates resisting scratches and impacts
Partitions Aluminum framing with 10mm clear toughened glass or double-glazed panels
Ceiling Grid Acoustic mineral fiber ceiling tiles or metal grid systems (Armstrong / Gyproc)
Data Cabling D-Link/Schneider CAT6 wiring routed through metal conduit trunks
