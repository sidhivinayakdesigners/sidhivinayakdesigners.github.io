Bespoke Furniture Design

Home / Services / Bespoke Furniture

Custom Furniture Fabrication & Natural Veneers

We manufacture customized loose furniture focusing on high craftsmanship and precise layouts. Fabricated fully in-house by our carpenters, each item blends aesthetic texture with long-term structural strength.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Dining Assemblies: Customized wood dining tables, heavy table bases, veneered/glass table tops, and cushioned chairs.
  • Sideboards & Consoles: Credenzas, foyer storage units, drawer organizers, and designer mirror frames.
  • Bar Cabinets: Accent bar units with overhead wine glass racks, integrated ledges, and mirror backing details.
  • Study & Office Desks: Space-saving corner tables, attached files cabinet pedestals, and library book racks.
  • Finishes & Polishing: Premium melamine coatings, natural PU wood polish, high-gloss polyester coatings, and hand-rubbed wax details.

Material Specifications

Component Furniture Specific Materials
Wood Core 18mm MR Grade IS:303 Blockboard or Commercial Plywood
Solid Wood Seasoned Teak wood or Steam Beech wood framing details
Veneer Layer 4mm natural teak, oak, or walnut veneers (CenturyVeneers)
Hardware Tracks Full-extension slide guides, concealed cabinet hinges (Ebco / Hettich)
Polish Coating Polyurethane (PU) polish coats for high scratch resistance (Asian Paints Woodtech)
