FAQs On Bathroom Designs

Find detailed answers regarding layouts, materials, space optimization, and customization.

A functional bathroom should include: A toilet and sink with vanity, Shower area or bathtub, Anti-skid flooring for safety, Storage units like cabinets and niches, Proper lighting and ventilation.

The right design depends on: Available space, Storage needs, Budget and style preference.

Popular modern bathroom trends include: Floating vanities and hidden storage, Minimalist layouts with clean lines, Neutral tones with accent tiles, Backlit mirrors and ambient lighting.

Small bathrooms can be highly functional with smart design: Wall-mounted vanities to free up floor space, Built-in niches and recessed shelves instead of bulky cabinets, Pocket or sliding doors to replace swing doors and save space, Corner units to utilise unused angles in the bathroom.

To create a premium look: Use marble or high-quality tiles, Add glass shower partitions, Choose gold or matte black fixtures, Install designer lighting.

Efficient bathroom storage options include: Vanity units with drawers for everyday essentials, Wall-mounted cabinets to free up floor space, Mirror cabinets with hidden storage, Shower niches for toiletries within the shower area, Ladder shelves for towels and décor without taking up floor space.

Popular bathroom colour combinations include: White and beige for a clean look, Grey and white for a modern feel, Black and white for contrast, Pastels for a soft and calming vibe.

The cost depends on: Bathroom size and layout, Materials such as tiles, fittings, and finishes, Storage and personalisation level.
