Gypsum Ceiling & Designer Light Alignment

We deliver modern false ceiling configurations using top-tier gypsum plasterboard coupled with custom structural framing and layout plans. Our ambient light schemes enhance interior volume, adding luxury depth to living spaces and bedrooms.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Gypsum Ceilings: Saint-Gobain Gyproc board, GI framing anchors, custom alignment steps, and clean joints finishing.
  • Cove Lighting: Concealed LED strip light setups, producing soft, indirect lighting across room perimeters.
  • Linear Profile LEDs: Modern recessed profile aluminum channel lights for clean geometric styling.
  • Track & Spotlights: Directed COB spotlights focused on paintings, artifacts, or specific workspace panels.
  • Chandelier Reinforcement: Heavy ceiling mounts and dedicated wiring safety loops for large centerpieces.

Material Specifications
