Premium Painting & Textures

Home / Services / Painting & Textures

Smooth Emulsions & Custom Accent Walls

We deliver professional wall preparation and premium painting services. Using top-tier emulsions and texture techniques, we guarantee crack-free finishing and clean sheen levels.

What's Included in the Scope

  • Putty Prep: Two coats of acrylic wall putty, precise sand smoothing, and crack filling.
  • Primer Base: High-grip water-based interior priming coats for even paint absorption.
  • Premium Emulsions: At least two coats of premium interior emulsions with high scrub resistance and rich colors.
  • Texture Highlights: Metallic, stucco, sandstorm, or customized spatula textures for main accent panels.
  • Wallpaper Fittings: High-density non-woven vinyl wallpaper measuring, trimming, and seamless paste mounting.

Material Specifications

Component Painting Materials Specs
Wall Putty Birla White or JK Pro-Plus Acrylic Wall Putty
Base Primer Decoprime water-based interior wall primer (Asian Paints)
Top Coat Paint Royale Luxury Emulsion / Royale Aspira (Asian Paints) or Velvet Touch (Dulux)
Texture Coatings Royale Play metallic/non-metallic special effect textures
Wallpaper Adhesive Heavy-duty starch-based wallpaper adhesive (Henkel / Bartoline) for moisture protection
